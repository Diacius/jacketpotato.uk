How not to do web design

This post is partially to teach you what not to do when designing a website, as well as to poke fun at awful design decisions.

Indistinguishible Highlight Colours

This is mainly an accessibility issue, but many people (including me), use their keyboard to navigate websites, which this makes infuriating.

You should be able to tab through an entire webpage knowing exactly which element is selected, and the page shouldn't have fake buttons, where there are clickable items on the page that aren't links or buttons in the HTML.
