What is a kibibyte?

Kilo,Mega,and Giga are metric prefixes. This means that they work in factors in ten.
However, computers work in binary, meaning they need units in multiples of 8 This is why the units kibibyte, mebibyte, and gibibyte exist. 1KiB = 1024 Bytes, instead of 1000 Bytes
